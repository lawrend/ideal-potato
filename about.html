Hi There!


My name is Saba Omidvar and I am a licensed Marriage and Family Therapist (LMFT) and have been practicing in the San Francisco Bay Area since 2014. I offer evidence-based, culturally-responsive psychotherapy services to teens, adults, couples and families with diverse cultural and diagnostic needs.

I do not believe in a one-size-fits-all approach, and work with each client to create an individualized treatment plan to address their concerns.

I graduated from the University of California, Berkeley with a Bachelor of Arts and received my Masters Degree from Palo Alto University. I have been intensively trained in Dialectical Behavioral Therapy (DBT) and DBT Physical Education.

I specialize in working with relationship and emotional regulation problems in teens, couples and adults. In addition, I have extensive experience working with blended families and co-parenting issues. I also have expertise working with loved ones of people with severe mental illness and can offer services for families touched by severe mental illness.

Please reach out to schedule a free consultation to discuss your needs and see if we would be a good fit for each other.
