About Saba Omidvar, LMFT

Contact

Hi There!

I am called Saba!

My name is Saba Omidvar and I offer evidence based, culturally responsive psychotherapy services. I have extensive experience working with clients with diverse cultural and diagnostic needs and my approach is centered on using evidence based strategies to help you meet your goals. I do not believe in a one size fits all approach, and work with each client to create a highly individualized treatment plan to address their concerns.The modalities I most often utilize are cognitive behavioral, dialectical behavioral and mindfulness based approaches. I have been intensively trained in Dialectical Behavioral Therapy (DBT) and DBT PE. I specialize in working with relationship and emotional regulation problems in teens, couples and adults. In addition, I have extensive experience working with blended families and co-parenting issues. I also have expertise working with loved ones of people with severe mental illness and can offer services for families touched by severe mental illness.

Please reach out to schedule a free consultation to discuss your needs and see if we would be a good fit for each other.
